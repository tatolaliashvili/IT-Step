World of Warcraft (WoW) is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment, released on November 23, 2004. Set in the epic fantasy universe of Azeroth, players choose from diverse races and classes to explore vast open worlds, complete thrilling quests, engage in intense PvP and PvE battles, and build communities in a persistent online realm. With ten major expansions to date—including the current The War Within (2024) and the upcoming Midnight (2026) as part of The Worldsoul Saga—WoW has amassed over 100 million lifetime accounts, peaked at 12 million subscribers, and generated billions in revenue, remaining one of the most influential games in history.
